HOW WE'VE STARTED OUR JOURNEY

We believe in breaking down borders & building bridges in our community & around the world. We love coffee, food, baked goods, art & our home, Ypsilanti.

We aim to be more than just another coffee shop, we aspire to create a safe space for the community by providing coffee, comfort, and collaboration.
